Attendance Policy, Investment, and Committment:

Attendance:

Participants cannot miss more than one training weekend. Any hours missed must be made up with a lead trainer at the rate of $100/hr.

Hours must be made up within 30 days of missed weekend.

Certification:

Attendance and payment does not guarantee certification. All requirements must be met in order for certification to be issued including: attendance, financial obligations, competencies, practicum, and more.

Committment:

This is a big investment and it requires a significant amount of time, effort, and resources. Please consider your investment wisely and ensure that you have made sufficient accomodations to support your success. (travel plans, child care, financial obligations, etc)

Investment:

Once accepted a deposit of $450 will secure your spot and will be applied to the total price of training.

Payment is expected in full by July 31st, 2020. If not paid in full by this date participant will be dismissed from the program and forfeit any prior payment as well as certification.

All payment plans must be discussed and approved by Angel Coughlin prior to start of training

Refunds:

Full refund (minus $100 processing fee) if you cancel at least 30 days prior to start of program.

No refund awarded if cancellation happens less than 30 days prior to the start of the program, if student is dismissed due to non payment, or student withdraws during the training.
