Attendance

Participants cannot miss more than one training weekend. Any hours missed must be made up with a lead trainer at the rate of $100/hr. ​Hours must be made up within 30 days of missed weekend.

Certification

Attendance and payment does not guarantee certification. All requirements must be met in order for certification to be issued including: attendance, financial obligations, competencies, practicum, and more.
